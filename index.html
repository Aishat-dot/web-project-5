2SLGBTQIA+


What each letter represents

2S Two-Spirit - Indigenous North American identity
L Lesbian - A woman attracted to other women.
G Gay - Someone(Typically men) attracted to the same gender.
B Bisexual - Someone attracted to more than one gender.
T Transgender - A person who's gender is different that what they were assigned at birth.
Q Queer - A person who does not identity with any of the other labels.
I Intersex - Someone whose chromosomes, hormonal makeup, or anatomy fall outside the categories of male or female.
A Asexual - Someone who does not feel sexual attraction towards individuals.
+ Plus - Additional identities not named in the acronym.
